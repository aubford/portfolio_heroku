.Gifs Against Humanity

Multiplayer Web Game

.Gifs Against Humanity is a 5 player websocket-heavy multiplayer real-time card game. It is based on the popular party game Cards Against Humanity, where people answer queries using cards with funny phrases on them. In my game, the players' cards are instead .gifs from the Giphy API. Each player gets a unique "hand" of cards and chooses one to put on the board. Players take turns being the "selector" who chooses the funniest card as the winner, giving the card's owner a point. Players are grouped into an infinite number of unique rooms with 5 players each. This was my first web app, built only three months after writing my first line of code.

Check out the project HERE . To play the game, open 5 tabs to simulate the five players required to begin.

VariAlt

Android Flight Computer Application

VariAlt is an Android flight computer app for hang gliding and paragliding. See vertical velocity, altitude and other flight data in real time and store it to track performance. Flight path can be viewed on Google Maps.
